The Rules

  1. No non-product people. This keeps the conversation real.
  2. No recruiters, consultants, vendors or agencies. These folks are important, but their presence changes the dynamic.
  3. Participants must have product leader level responsibility: CPO, VP/SVP Product, Director or equivalent.
  4. No talking about the lunch outside of the lunch.
  5. No photos or social media.
  6. All conversations at the lunches and in the CPO Lunches community are strictly off the record.
  7. Unsubscribe at any time.
